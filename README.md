Project Overview
The objective of this project is to analyze pizza sales performance and extract actionable business insights through:
Data Preparation & EDA (Python):
Cleaned and transformed raw sales data, handled missing values, and prepared dataset for analysis.
Data Analysis (SQL):
Calculated key KPIs such as total revenue, average order value, total orders, and identified top-performing products, categories, and sizes.
Data Visualization (Power BI):
Developed an interactive dashboard to visualize sales trends, customer ordering behavior, and product performance.
Reporting & Insights:
Summarized key findings and provided business insights to improve sales performance and decision-making.

📊 Key Insights
Classic category generated the highest revenue
Large size pizzas contributed most to total sales
Sales peak during weekends
A small number of products drive the majority of revenue
Solution & Recommendations
Based on the analysis, several actionable strategies can be proposed to improve business performance:
Optimize product mix:Focus on promoting high-revenue categories such as Classic pizzas while reviewing underperforming items to improve or remove them.
Leverage peak sales periods:Since sales peak during weekends, businesses can introduce special promotions, combo deals, or targeted marketing campaigns to maximize revenue during these periods.
Promote high-demand sizes:Large-size pizzas contribute the most to total sales. Offering bundle deals or discounts on large sizes can further boost revenue.
Improve marketing strategy:Identify top-performing products and use them in promotional campaigns to attract more customers and increase conversion rates.
Customer behavior insights:Analyze ordering patterns to design personalized offers and improve customer retention.
